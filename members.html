More than meetings

Friendship is part of the deal

There is more to Rotary than service projects and Thursday breakfasts. The friendships and the camaraderie are a big part of why people stay. We work together, we celebrate together, and we have a good time doing it.

Our leadership team keeps the club running and our projects on track. You can meet the current board on our about page.
