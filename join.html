Join us

Come as a guest. Stay as a friend.

There is no cost and no commitment to visit. Drop in on a Thursday meeting, meet the members, and see if our club feels like a fit for you.

Why people join

What you get out of it

Purpose

Spend your time on work that helps real people in your own community.

Friendship

Meet people from all walks of life who share your values.

Growth

Build leadership skills and connect with our worldwide Rotary network.

Impact

Be part of projects that reach from Oldsmar to the other side of the world.

What to expect

A typical Thursday

We meet every Thursday from 8:00 to 9:00 AM at Eve's Family Restaurant, 3150 Tampa Rd in Oldsmar. You can also join us on Zoom if that is easier. There is breakfast, a short program, updates on our projects, and plenty of friendly conversation.

Come as you are. Visitors are always welcome, and there is never any pressure to join.

How to join

Three easy steps

1

Visit a meeting

Email us and let us know you would like to come. We will greet you and introduce you around.

2

Get to know us

Come a few times, meet the members, and see the projects we are working on right now.

3

Apply

When you are ready, fill out a simple membership application and we will welcome you in.
