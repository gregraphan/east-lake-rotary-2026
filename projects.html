Recent work

What we have been up to

Reading benches for schools

With the Miller Family Foundation, we deliver reading benches, books, and baskets to schools across the area, including Chi Chi Rodriguez Academy, Highpoint, and Kings Highway Elementary.

Free reading program

We partner with Be Reading to give local families free reading activities for children in grades K through 6. Thousands of activities, real gains, and no cost to families.

Set up a free reading account →

Habitat for Humanity

Our members have rolled up their sleeves on Habitat builds, helping families in our area move into a home of their own.

Shred-a-thon & World Polio Day

Our shred events at the East Lake Library raise funds and awareness in Rotary's worldwide fight to end polio.

Owl-o-Ween at Brooker Creek

A family favorite. We help bring the community together for a fun fall event at Brooker Creek Preserve.

Food packing, drives & the pantry

We help pack meals, support the East Lake Library's & Oldsmar Cares' food pantries, and give to animal shelters like the Humane Society.

Over the years our giving and volunteer hours have gone to organizations that do great work in our community.

Our biggest event

Cruisin' The Park Car Show

Once a year we fill the park with classic cars, music, food, and families for our signature fundraiser. It is the engine behind a lot of the projects on this page.
