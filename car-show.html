Our signature event

Cruisin' The Park

Our annual charity car show and fall festival. Classic cars, live music, food, and a great day out for the whole family, all to raise money for our community work.

  1. Home
  2. Car Show

A community tradition

For nearly two decades, Cruisin' The Park has been our biggest fundraiser of the year. Show cars line the park, music plays, vendors and food trucks set up, and families come out for the afternoon. Every dollar raised goes back into the community through our grants and service projects.

  • Annual spring event. Our 19th annual show was held April 18, 2026. Thank you to everyone who came out.
  • In the park, Oldsmar FL. A full fall festival atmosphere with cars, crafts, and community.
  • Free to attend. Spectators get in free. Entry fees apply for show vehicles.
