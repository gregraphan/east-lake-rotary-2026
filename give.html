Your gift goes to work close to home

We are an all-volunteer club, so the money you give does not pay salaries. It buys reading benches, fills food pantries, and funds the local projects that need it most.

Ways to help

More than one way to give

Make a donation

A one-time or recurring gift of any size helps fund our community projects. Give securely online, or mail a check to our PO box.

Sponsor the car show

Cruisin' The Park is our biggest fundraiser. Business sponsorships put your name in front of the community and directly fund our work. Become a sponsor online.

Give your time

Money is only part of it. Volunteers are always needed for projects and events. Time is one of the most valuable gifts you can give.

Where it goes

Real things, for real neighbors

The Rotary Club of East Lake Sunrise, together with the Miller Family Foundation, provides reading benches, books, and baskets to schools throughout our area. That is one example of what giving makes possible.

Your support also reaches the Oldsmar Cares Food Pantry, local clinics and shelters, youth programs, and Rotary's global effort to end polio. We keep it local first, and we keep it honest.
